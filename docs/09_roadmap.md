# Arkova Multi-Phase Product Roadmap

## Phase I (Current)
Core anchoring and verification platform. Identity onboarding and basic lifecycle management.

## Phase II (Core Deliverables)
- **Legal Document Attestations (Phase II)**:
  - Anchoring and lifecycle tracking for legal document evidence manifests and related compliance attestations.
- Scaling API limits and enterprise org management.

## Phase III (Core Deliverables)
- **Audit Infrastructure (Phase III)**:
  - Evidence bundles, auditor-ready exports, and continuous audit preparation workflows to reduce audit time and cost.
- Multi-region compliance clusters.
